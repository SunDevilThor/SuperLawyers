    Elder Law

    Are you a senior citizen, facing a legal issue that is unique to your age group?

    Or do you have a loved one who you fear is being abused or taken advantage of as a result of their age?

    Find help with Super Lawyers.

    Elder law attorneys primarily advise a range of clients on laws and issues that impact senior citizens.

    Examples of the broad services they provide include representing seniors and their families in cases of abuse in health or long term care institutions, defending employees of institutions serving senior citizens, guiding families in relation to a family member’s incapacitating health issues, or assisting senior citizens in relation to government provided benefits.

    This practice comprises both litigation and non-litigious matters.

    Use Super Lawyers to hire a local attorney to help you with any legal issues affecting the elderly.
